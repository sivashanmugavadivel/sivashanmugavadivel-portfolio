# Spicy Mushroom Masala Omelet – Easy High-Protein Breakfast

If you're looking for a quick, flavorful, and protein-packed breakfast, this Mushroom Masala Omelet is a perfect choice. Bold spices, earthy mushrooms, and fluffy eggs come together in minutes — a simple yet satisfying way to start your day.

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients
- 1 cup white button mushrooms, sliced
- 3 eggs
- 1/4 cup red onion, chopped
- 1/4 cup green bell pepper, chopped
- 3–4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp oil (olive oil or any cooking oil)
- 1–2 tsp lemon juice
- Salt to taste
- Black pepper to taste

### Masala Mix
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
- 1/4 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp coriander powder

---

## Instructions

### Step 1: Marinate the Mushrooms
Wash and slice the mushrooms. Mix with chili powders, garam masala, coriander powder, salt, black pepper, and lemon juice. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes so the spices soak in.

### Step 2: Cook the Mushroom Filling
Heat a pan on medium flame. Sauté onion and garlic for 1–2 minutes. Add bell pepper and cook for another minute. Add the marinated mushrooms and cook for 3–5 minutes until the moisture releases and the filling is fragrant.

### Step 3: Prepare the Egg Mixture
Crack 3 eggs into a bowl. Add a pinch of salt and black pepper. Beat until smooth and slightly frothy.

### Step 4: Make the Omelet
Spread the mushroom mixture evenly in the pan. Pour the beaten eggs over the top. Cook on low-medium heat until the bottom sets. Flip or fold the omelet and cook for another 1–2 minutes until fully done.
